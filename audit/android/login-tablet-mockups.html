Bizarre CRM — Android tablet login flow

Pixel Tablet landscape (1280×800) · Material 3 Expressive α · MEDIUM window class. Three-pane adaptive layout: NavigationRail (80dp) + center detail (~60%) + supporting pane (~30%). This is not an upscaled phone — the supporting pane carries context that lives in a sheet on compact. Solid surfaces with layered scrims; zero glassmorphism per Theme.kt §1.4. Each frame appears in both Dark and Light variants from BizarreCrmTheme. Verbatim copy is sourced from LoginScreen.kt ("Connect to Your Shop", "Sign In", "Two-Factor Authentication", "Create your admin account", etc.).
