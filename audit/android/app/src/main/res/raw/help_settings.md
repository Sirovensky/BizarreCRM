# Settings

## Profile and security

- **Edit Profile** — update your name, email, and avatar.
- **Security** — enable biometric unlock, set or change your PIN, change your
  password, and manage 2FA factors.
- **Active Sessions** — review and revoke sessions on other devices.

## Notifications

Configure which events trigger push, in-app, email, or SMS notifications. Tap
**Settings → Notifications** to manage per-event overrides.

## Appearance

- **Dashboard Density** — choose Compact, Comfortable, or Spacious card layout.
- **Theme** — system default, light, or dark mode. Dynamic color follows your
  wallpaper on Android 12+.

## Language

Set your preferred language in **Settings → Language**. The app restarts to
apply the change.

## Hardware

Pair receipt printers, label printers, and the BlockChyp payment terminal from
**Settings → Hardware**.

## Data

- **Import** — import RepairDesk or CSV data (admin only).
- **Export** — download tickets, customers, and invoices as CSV.
- **Clear cache** — remove locally cached data (does not delete server data).

## Training Mode

Enable a sandbox environment to practice workflows without touching real data.
Demo customers, tickets, and inventory are pre-loaded.
