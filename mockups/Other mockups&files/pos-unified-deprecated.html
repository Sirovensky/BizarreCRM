POS — Point of Sale

Unified mockup. Five platform variants × dark + light. Same workflows everywhere — larger screens get higher density. Three design languages: Web = Bizarre Workstation (PC, mouse + keyboard, no glass) · iOS = Liquid Glass (iPhone + iPad) · Android = M3 Expressive (Phone + Tablet).

5 Platforms · 3 design languages
· Web — Bizarre Workstation
· iPad — Liquid Glass
· iPhone — Liquid Glass
· Android Tablet — M3 Expressive
· Android Phone — M3 Expressive
15 Screens × 5 platforms × 2 modes = 150 frames
One continuous POS flow: Entry · Categories · Manufacturers · Devices · Parts & Repairs · Search · Custom Part · Symptoms · Details · Pre-damage · Diagnostic · Quote · Sign · Tender · Receipt. Repair check-in is just the path when a device drops off — same flow, different middle.
📐
Layout: per-screen comparison rows. Each screen renders in all 5 platforms side-by-side (Web · iPad · iPhone · Android Tablet · Android Phone) for both Dark + Light. Scroll horizontally within each row to compare.

Web · Bizarre Workstation

1320 × 800 · React + Tailwind

Persistent left sidebar, top header bar. POS lives as a page within the CRM — same shell as Dashboard. Content area splits: catalog browsing left, cart panel right (always visible).
