# Customers

## Adding a customer

Tap **+** on the Customers screen or let the check-in flow create one during
ticket creation. A phone number or email is required for SMS and email
notifications.

## Searching customers

Use the search bar at the top of the Customers list. You can search by name,
phone, or email. Scan a customer card barcode with the camera icon.

## Customer notes

Open the customer record and tap **Notes** to add private notes visible only
to staff. Notes support plain text and are timestamped automatically.

## Health score and LTV tier

The health score ring shows engagement signals (visit frequency, spend, referrals).
The LTV tier (Bronze / Silver / Gold / VIP) is calculated by the server based
on lifetime spend thresholds set in admin settings.

## Merging duplicates

Duplicate merging is done from the web CRM. Open the customer record on the
desktop and use **Actions → Merge**.

## Opt-out management

A customer who replies STOP to an SMS is automatically opted out of marketing
messages. The opt-out flag is visible on the customer record and cannot be
overridden from the mobile app.
