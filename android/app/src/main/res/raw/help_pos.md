# Point of Sale (POS)

## Starting a sale

Tap **POS** in the bottom navigation. Search or scan items to add them to the
cart. You can also attach the sale to an existing ticket.

## Payment methods

The POS supports cash, card (via BlockChyp terminal or tap-to-pay), store credit,
gift cards, and split payments across multiple methods.

## Splitting a payment

Tap **Split** in the cart to divide the total across two or more payment methods.

## Receipts

After a successful transaction, a receipt can be printed (if a receipt printer
is paired), emailed, or SMS'd to the customer.

## Discounts and tax

Tap a line item to apply a discount or change the tax class. Tax rates are
configured in **Settings → POS & Payment**.

## Refunds from POS history

Open **POS → Sales History**, find the transaction, and tap **Refund**. A
manager PIN may be required depending on the refund amount.

## Gift cards

Sell a new gift card by adding the **Gift Card** product to the cart. To redeem,
tap **Gift Card** as the payment method and scan or enter the card number.
