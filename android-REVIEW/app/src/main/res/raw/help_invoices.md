# Invoices

## Creating an invoice

Invoices are generated automatically when a ticket is marked **Ready** or from
the POS after a sale. You can also tap **+ New Invoice** on the Invoices screen.

## Payment status

- **Draft** — not yet sent to the customer.
- **Sent** — emailed or SMS-linked to the customer.
- **Paid** — full payment received.
- **Partial** — partial payment recorded.
- **Overdue** — past the due date with an outstanding balance.

## Applying payments

Open the invoice and tap **Record Payment**. Choose the payment method
(cash, card, store credit, etc.) and enter the amount.

## Refunds

Tap **Refund** on a paid invoice. Refunds above the configured threshold require
a manager PIN. Refunds can go back to the original tender or as store credit.

## Sending a payment link

Tap **Send Link** on an unpaid invoice to generate a customer-facing payment
page. The customer pays via card without needing the app.

## Exporting invoices

Use **Settings → Data → Export** to download invoices as a CSV or PDF batch.
