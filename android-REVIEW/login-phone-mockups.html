Bizarre CRM — Android phone login flow (Material 3 Expressive α)

Pixel 9 Pro compact mockups (412×916) for the multi-step login flow. Built on material3 1.5.0-alpha18 with MotionScheme.expressive(): LargeFlexibleTopAppBar, LinearWavyProgressIndicator, shape-morphing buttons, MaterialShapes cookie hero, FAB Menu, Split Button. Solid surfaces only — Android forbids backdrop-filter glassmorphism per Theme.kt §1.4. iOS counterpart lives at ios/pos-iphone-mockups.html.
